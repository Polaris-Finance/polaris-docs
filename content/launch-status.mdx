## What you need

- **An Ethereum wallet** — any standard EOA or smart-account wallet.
- **For Public Testnet 1:** Sepolia ETH for gas, plus test WETH/reserve tokens from the app faucet when it's enabled for your wallet. Testnet assets have no monetary value.
- **For production later:** ETH on Ethereum mainnet — the only asset you need to begin; Polaris converts it to [pETH](/peth) for you.

No KYC, no whitelist, no offchain account.

## The basic flow

In one line: **deposit ETH ⇒ receive pETH ⇒ open a trove and mint a pAsset ⇒ optionally deposit to the Stability Pool to earn yield.** On Public Testnet 1 this uses Sepolia test assets; in production it uses Ethereum mainnet once this page says mainnet is live.

1. **Deposit ETH or test assets.** On Public Testnet 1, mint mock WETH/reserve tokens from the app faucet when available. In production later, you start from mainnet ETH.
2. **Receive pETH from the [bonding curve](/peth).** Your input is swapped for pETH at the curve's current price. pETH is an ETH proxy with a floor price that only rises, and the only collateral Polaris accepts.
3. **Open a [trove](/minting/open-a-trove) and mint a pAsset.** Post pETH as collateral, then choose your pAsset. pUSD and pGOLD are Testnet-active; pCHF and pBigMac are illustrative catalog examples. The pAsset lands in your wallet; the debt stays in your trove — see [the catalog](/minting/passet-catalog).
4. **Optionally, earn yield.** Deposit your pAsset into the [Stability Pool](/yield) for a share of borrower interest plus discounted pETH from liquidations. Or hold, LP, or use it anywhere in DeFi.

## Before any transaction

Do not connect a wallet or sign a transaction unless all relevant checks pass:

- For Public Testnet 1, the app URL is exactly [https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/](https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/).
- For production/mainnet, wait until this page publishes a production app URL.
- The wallet network matches the intended environment: Sepolia for Public Testnet 1, Ethereum mainnet only after production launch.
- The contract address appears in [Contracts & Addresses](/resources/contracts), [Public Testnet 1](/resources/testnet), or the official deployment manifest you are using.
- Explorer source and ABI match the documented contract.
- The relevant product page has final values for the parameters your action depends on.
- Audit and disclosure status are clear on [Audits & Security](/resources/audits-security).

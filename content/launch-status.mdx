## Network truth

Public Testnet 1 runs on **Sepolia (`11155111`)** and may use mock assets, mock oracles, redeployable contracts, and testnet-only parameters. It is for testing behavior, not for valuing production risk. The current app URL, active testnet pAssets, manifests, addresses, and Sepolia parameters live on [Public Testnet 1](/resources/testnet).

Production Polaris is intended for **Ethereum mainnet (`1`)**, but mainnet app and contract addresses are not live or published yet. Any frontend, token, contract address, airdrop, support message, or bounty page claiming production status before it is linked here should be treated as unverified.

## Before any transaction

Do not connect a wallet or sign a transaction unless all relevant checks pass:

- For Public Testnet 1, the app URL is exactly [https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/](https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/).
- For production/mainnet, wait until this page publishes a production app URL.
- The wallet network matches the intended environment: Sepolia for Public Testnet 1, Ethereum mainnet only after production launch.
- The contract address appears in [Contracts & Addresses](/resources/contracts), [Public Testnet 1](/resources/testnet), or the official deployment manifest you are using.
- Explorer source and ABI match the documented contract.
- The relevant product page has final values for the parameters your action depends on.
- Audit and disclosure status are clear on [Audits & Security](/resources/audits-security).

## Page labels used in these docs

- **Conceptual only** means the page explains the model and risks, but does not give enough live parameters to sign safely.
- **Testnet-actionable** means you can try the flow on Sepolia with test assets and testnet manifests.
- **Mainnet-actionable later** means the page needs production app links, verified addresses, final parameters, and audit status before use with real value.

For phishing and user-safety checks, continue to [Safety and Verification](/paths/safety-verification). For the intended deployment shape, see [Contracts & Addresses](/resources/contracts).

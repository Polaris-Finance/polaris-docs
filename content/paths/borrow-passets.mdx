# Borrow Polaris pAssets

**Who this is for:** anyone who wants to mint pUSD, pGOLD, or another pAsset against pETH collateral and actively manage that position.

**Prerequisites:** an Ethereum wallet, test assets on Sepolia for Public Testnet 1 or ETH on Ethereum mainnet after production launch, and a working grasp of overcollateralization and liquidation. If those terms are new, read [Core Concepts](/core-concepts) first.

> **Launch status:** Borrowing is testnet-actionable on Sepolia only when using the official Public Testnet 1 manifests. Production borrowing is not mainnet-actionable until [Polaris Launch Status](/launch-status) publishes the app URL, production addresses, final collateral thresholds, and audit status. Do not connect to a frontend or contract that claims to be Polaris unless it is linked from these docs or another official Polaris channel.

> **Terminology:** In the app, **Borrow** is the task label. Docs call a pAsset debt position a **trove**; contracts may call the mechanism a **CDP**. **Reserve loans** are separate WETH loans against [fpETH](/peth/split), not pAsset troves.

6. [Liquidations](/redemptions-liquidations/liquidations) and [Recovery Mode](/redemptions-liquidations/recovery-mode) — the failure states that decide how conservatively to size your position.

## What to verify at launch

- The official app URL.
- The intended network: Sepolia (`11155111`) for Public Testnet 1, or Ethereum mainnet (`1`) after production launch.
- The pETH, pAsset, trove manager, and Stability Pool addresses.
- Final minimum collateralization, liquidation, redemption, and fee parameters for the pAsset you mint.
- Audit reports and verified contract source.

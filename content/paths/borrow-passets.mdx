# Borrow pAssets

**Who this is for:** anyone who wants to mint pUSD, pGOLD, or another pAsset against pETH collateral and actively manage that position.

**Prerequisites:** an Ethereum wallet, ETH on Ethereum mainnet, and a working grasp of overcollateralization and liquidation. If those terms are new, read [Core Concepts](/core-concepts) first.

> **Launch status:** The public app URL, deployed addresses, final collateral thresholds, and exact UI labels are not published yet. Do not connect to a frontend or contract that claims to be Polaris unless it is linked from these docs or another official Polaris channel at launch.

## Reading sequence

Read these in order — each step builds context the next one assumes.

1. [Getting Started](/getting-started) — confirms your wallet, network, and launch status so you avoid an unverified deployment.
2. [The Bonding Curve](/peth/bonding-curve) — pETH is the *only* collateral Polaris accepts; you must hold it before borrowing.
3. [Minting pAssets](/minting/minting-passets) — explains the CDP model and the peg mechanics that determine your interest rate.
4. [Open a Trove](/minting/open-a-trove) — the conceptual flow for posting collateral and minting your pAsset.
5. [Managing Your Trove](/minting/managing-your-trove) — how collateralization, debt, and interest move over time, and how to stay clear of liquidation.
6. [Liquidations](/redemptions-liquidations/liquidations) and [Recovery Mode](/redemptions-liquidations/recovery-mode) — the failure states that decide how conservatively to size your position.

## What to verify at launch

- The official app URL.
- Ethereum mainnet (chain ID 1).
- The pETH, pAsset, trove manager, and Stability Pool addresses.
- Final minimum collateralization, liquidation, redemption, and fee parameters for the pAsset you mint.
- Audit reports and verified contract source.

# Hold or Use pETH

**Who this is for:** anyone who wants to hold pETH for its rising floor, use it as collateral, or understand the asset before borrowing against it.

**Prerequisites:** ETH on Ethereum and a wallet. No trove or pAsset is required to hold pETH; borrowing is optional and comes later.

> **Launch status:** The pETH contract address, bonding-curve deployment, external integrations, and final curve parameters are pending publication at launch. Do not trust unofficial addresses.

## Reading sequence

Read these in order to understand pETH and its downside bound before relying on it.

1. [The Bonding Curve](/peth/bonding-curve) — how ETH deposits become pETH, and where your exit liquidity comes from.
2. [The Floor Price](/peth/floor-price) — the mathematically enforced downside bound in ETH terms; this is your worst case.
3. [Minting pAssets](/minting/minting-passets) — only if you may use pETH inside a trove; explains what changes when pETH becomes collateral.
4. [Where the Yield Comes From](/yield/yield-sources) — how bonding-curve and conversion activity raises the floor for pETH holders.
5. [Risk Disclosure](/resources/risk-disclosure) — read before using pETH as collateral anywhere, inside or outside Polaris.

# Convert, Stake, and Future-Lock POLAR

**Who this is for:** long-term participants who want to acquire POLAR through conversion, use current POLAR Staking where available, and understand forthcoming vePOLAR locking.

**Prerequisites:** pETH to burn in a conversion auction, and acceptance that conversion is one-way — you cannot un-burn pETH. To understand what your burn contributes, read [The Floor Price](/peth/floor-price) first.

> **Launch status:** Conversion and current staking are testnet-actionable only where Sepolia artifacts exist. Production conversion, staking, and vePOLAR locking are not mainnet-actionable until [Polaris Launch Status](/launch-status) publishes the official app, POLAR, staking, and vePOLAR addresses, lock durations, voting process, final parameter bounds, and audit status. Do not use an unofficial conversion, staking, or locking interface.

## POLAR Staking vs vePOLAR

POLAR Staking is the current app staking/rewards surface where exposed. vePOLAR is forthcoming vote-escrow locking. Once live, vePOLAR can steward bounded parameters, vote on permitted onboarding decisions, and receive a stewarded share of protocol yield. It cannot upgrade contracts, change the bonding curve, change liquidation or redemption logic, create emissions gauges, or mint POLAR except through pETH conversion.

## What to verify at launch

- The official POLAR, conversion auction, and vePOLAR addresses.
- The official POLAR Staking address and claim path, if staking is exposed.
- Verified source and ABI for each contract.
- Final lock duration choices and vote-power decay rules.
- Final proposal, voting, quorum, timelock, and execution rules.
- The hardcoded parameter bounds for any proposal you vote on.

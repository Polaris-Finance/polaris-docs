# Convert and Lock POLAR on Polaris

**Who this is for:** long-term participants who want to acquire POLAR through conversion, lock it as vePOLAR, and steer the protocol within its hardcoded bounds.

**Prerequisites:** pETH to burn in a conversion auction, and acceptance that conversion is one-way — you cannot un-burn pETH. To understand what your burn contributes, read [The Floor Price](/peth/floor-price) first.

> **Launch status:** Conversion and locking are testnet-actionable only where Sepolia artifacts exist. Production conversion and locking are not mainnet-actionable until [Polaris Launch Status](/launch-status) publishes the official app, POLAR and vePOLAR addresses, lock durations, voting process, final parameter bounds, and audit status. Do not use an unofficial conversion or locking interface.

## Reading sequence

Read these in order — understand the token and the irreversible burn before committing pETH.

1. [The POLAR Token](/polar/polar-token) — POLAR's role and why, after the initial distribution, new POLAR can be minted *only* by burning pETH.
2. [Conversion Auctions](/polar/conversion-auctions) — the mechanism and its risks; read before burning any pETH to mint POLAR.
3. [Participate in a Conversion](/polar/participate-in-conversion) — the conceptual user flow for a conversion.
4. [vePOLAR](/stewardship/vepolar) — locking durations, the rights locking grants, and its limitations.
5. [Stewardship, not Governance](/stewardship/stewardship) — the hardcoded bounds your vote operates within; read before voting on any proposal.

## What vePOLAR can and cannot do

vePOLAR can steward bounded parameters, vote on permitted onboarding decisions, and receive a stewarded share of protocol yield. (Staker yield routes as primary interest, in pAsset terms, via the protocol's interest router, not the Fee Router; the Fee Router instead buffers bonding-curve fees and distributes them to troves.) It cannot upgrade contracts, change the bonding curve, change liquidation or redemption logic, create emissions gauges, or mint POLAR except through pETH conversion.

## What to verify at launch

- The official POLAR, conversion auction, and vePOLAR addresses.
- Verified source and ABI for each contract.
- Final lock duration choices and vote-power decay rules.
- Final proposal, voting, quorum, timelock, and execution rules.
- The hardcoded parameter bounds for any proposal you vote on.

## What you need

- **An Ethereum wallet** — any standard EOA or smart-account wallet.
- **ETH on Ethereum mainnet** — the only asset you need to begin; Polaris converts it to [pETH](/peth/bonding-curve) for you.

That's the whole list. No KYC, no whitelist, no offchain account.

## The basic flow

In one line: **deposit ETH ⇒ receive pETH ⇒ open a trove and mint a pAsset ⇒ optionally deposit to the Stability Pool to earn yield.** Step by step:

1. **Deposit ETH.** Connect your wallet and deposit ETH into the protocol.
2. **Receive pETH from the [bonding curve](/peth/bonding-curve).** Your ETH is swapped for pETH at the curve's current price. pETH is an ETH proxy with a floor price that only rises, and the only collateral Polaris accepts.
3. **Open a [trove](/minting/open-a-trove) and mint a pAsset.** Post your pETH as collateral, then choose which pAsset to mint (pUSD, pGOLD, …) and how much. The pAsset lands in your wallet; the debt stays in your trove. See [the catalog](/minting/passet-catalog) for what you can mint.
4. **Optionally, earn yield.** Deposit your pAsset into the [Stability Pool](/yield/stability-pool) to earn a share of borrower interest plus discounted pETH from liquidations. This is optional — you can instead hold, LP, or use your pAsset anywhere in DeFi.

## Launch verification

Before you connect a wallet or sign a transaction, verify:

- The app URL is linked from this documentation or another official Polaris channel.
- The app is connected to Ethereum mainnet (chain ID 1).
- Contract addresses match [Contracts and Addresses](/resources/contracts).
- Verified source, ABIs, and audit status match [Contracts and Addresses](/resources/contracts) and [Audits and Security](/resources/audits-security).
- The product page you're following has been updated from conceptual flow to launch parameters.

# Troubleshooting

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 8, 2026" />

Use this page when the app, wallet, quote, balance, or transaction does not behave as expected. For conceptual questions rather than broken flows, see the [FAQ](/resources/faq).

Start from the known-good setup: Public Testnet 1 at [app.testnet.polarisfinance.io](https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io), Sepolia chain ID `11155111`, test assets only.

## Wrong network

**Symptom:** The app asks you to switch networks, actions are disabled, or wallet prompts mention another chain.

**Likely cause:** Wallet is not connected to Sepolia.

**Fix:** Switch the wallet network to Sepolia, chain ID `11155111`, then refresh the app.

**When not to proceed:** Do not sign if the wallet prompt shows mainnet or an unexpected chain.

**Related reference:** [Public Testnet 1 Artifacts](/resources/testnet)

## Wrong frontend

**Symptom:** The app looks unfamiliar, links differ, or wallet prompts do not match the expected action.

**Likely cause:** You are on the wrong frontend or a phishing copy.

**Fix:** Use [app.testnet.polarisfinance.io](https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io) for Public Testnet 1 and verify links from the docs.

**When not to proceed:** Do not connect or sign from an unverified URL.

**Related reference:** [Launch Status](/launch-status)

## Faucet or test-token shortage

**Symptom:** You cannot start because WETH, pETH, pAssets, POLAR, or Sepolia ETH are missing.

**Likely cause:** The wallet lacks test assets or gas.

**Fix:** Follow [Public Testnet Quickstart](/quickstart) for WETH, pETH, and pAssets; POLAR comes from [conversion](/using-app/advanced). Then refresh balances.

**When not to proceed:** Do not bridge or send real mainnet funds to testnet contracts.

**Related reference:** [Public Testnet 1 Artifacts](/resources/testnet)

## Missing balance

**Symptom:** The app shows a balance but the wallet does not, or the wallet shows a token the app does not.

**Likely cause:** Token is hidden in the wallet, the wrong account is connected, or the app state is stale.

**Fix:** Confirm the connected account, refresh the app, and import the token using the canonical address if needed.

**When not to proceed:** Do not import token addresses from chat links or search results.

**Related reference:** [Contracts & Addresses](/resources/testnet#contracts-and-addresses)

## Approval succeeded, action still fails

**Symptom:** Wallet shows an approval transaction succeeded, but the app still asks for approval or the main action fails.

**Likely cause:** Approval was for the wrong spender, wrong token, insufficient amount, or a different account.

**Fix:** Check spender, token, wallet account, and allowance. Then retry only from the correct action tab.

**When not to proceed:** Do not grant unlimited approvals to an unexpected spender.

**Related reference:** [Advanced](/using-app/advanced)

## Stale quote or changed output

**Symptom:** Swap or Zap output changes, minimum received differs, or the transaction reverts.

**Likely cause:** Price, liquidity, route, gas, or slippage changed before inclusion.

**Fix:** Refresh the quote, review the route again, and use a slippage setting you understand.

**When not to proceed:** Do not sign if the wallet prompt no longer matches the app quote.

**Related reference:** [Swap](/using-app/swap), [Zap](/using-app/zap)

## Borrow action disabled or reverted

**Symptom:** Borrow, repay, adjust, or close cannot be submitted.

**Likely cause:** Position health, collateral, debt amount, Recovery Mode, allowance, or price movement blocks the action.

**Fix:** Refresh position data, reduce risk, check collateral and debt amounts, and retry from the Borrow tab.

**When not to proceed:** Do not borrow if you do not understand the displayed liquidation exposure.

**Related reference:** [Borrow](/using-app/borrow), [Recovery Mode](/redemptions-liquidations/recovery-mode), [Risk Disclosure](/resources/risk-disclosure)

## Earn action unavailable

**Symptom:** Deposit, withdraw, claim, stake, or unstake is disabled.

**Likely cause:** No eligible balance, no claimable amount, current deployment limits, or wallet/network mismatch.

**Fix:** Confirm the selected Earn surface, wallet account, balances, and network.

**When not to proceed:** Do not treat APR estimates as guaranteed returns.

**Related reference:** [Earn](/using-app/earn), [Yield](/yield)

## Split or merge fails

**Symptom:** Split or Merge reverts, or Merge allows less than expected.

**Likely cause:** You lack pETH for Split or equal fpETH and vpETH balances for Merge.

**Fix:** Check both token balances and merge only up to the smaller leg balance.

**When not to proceed:** Do not hold only one leg unless you understand the changed exposure.

**Related reference:** [Split](/using-app/split), [pETH Split](/peth/split)

## Transaction pending, out of gas, or reverted

**Symptom:** Wallet shows pending for a long time, or the transaction fails after submission.

**Likely cause:** Sepolia congestion, low gas, state changed, or transaction simulation missed a revert.

**Fix:** Wait for the pending transaction to settle, then refresh app state before retrying.

**When not to proceed:** Do not repeatedly submit the same action without checking whether the first transaction changed balances, allowances, or positions.

**Related reference:** [Dashboard](/using-app)

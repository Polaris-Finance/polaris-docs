# 2-Minute Overview

Polaris is a **self-scaling stablecoin operating system** — trustless, immutable infrastructure for minting synthetic assets backed entirely by onchain collateral and yield. No T-bills. No CEXs. No counterparties.

## One primitive, three products

Everything flows from a single primitive: the **bonding curve**.

**pETH** is ETH deposited into a two-way bonding curve. It is the only collateral Polaris accepts. Its floor price — the minimum price at which you can always redeem it for ETH — can only rise. pETH is yield-bearing: swap fees accrue to the protocol and permanently raise that floor over time.

**pAssets** (pUSD, pGOLD, …) are synthetic assets minted against pETH via a CDP (Collateralized Debt Position). You lock pETH; the protocol mints a pAsset into your wallet. No counterparty holds your position. The logic is immutable.

**POLAR** is the stewardship token. Beyond a fixed supply minted once at launch, the only way to create more is to burn pETH in a conversion auction. Through stewardship, POLAR holders steer protocol parameters within the bounds the immutable contracts define — they do not control the immutable core.

## The flywheel

The three products compound:

![Polaris flywheel: adoption drives activity, activity generates fees, fees enable conversions, conversions raise the pETH floor, a higher floor strengthens collateral, stronger collateral enables more minting](/infographics/polaris-flywheel.svg)

> More pAsset activity → fees → conversions → higher pETH floor → stronger collateral → more minting.

Growth reinforces the system rather than diluting it.

## Why it is different

- **Immutable core.** Bonding curve, CDP logic, liquidations, and redemptions are fixed in code. No admin key.
- **Onchain-only yield.** Every yield source (borrower interest, swap fees, conversion gains) is internal to the protocol and grows with DeFi activity.
- **Guaranteed floor.** pETH's floor can only rise. Your maximum downside on pETH is mathematically bounded and shrinks over time.

That is the whole system. Three products, one flywheel, one immutable core.

# vePOLAR

**vePOLAR is vote-escrowed POLAR.** Lock [POLAR](/polar/polar-token) and receive vePOLAR: the right to steward bounded parameters and participate in the protocol's edge decisions.

> **Current status:** The vePOLAR locking interface, contract address, lock durations, vote-power decay, unlock rules, proposal thresholds, and final yield split are pending audited deployment. Do not lock POLAR through an unofficial interface.

## What vePOLAR does

```
lock POLAR -> receive vePOLAR -> steward bounded parameters + receive routed yield
```

vePOLAR holders are **stewards, not governors**. vePOLAR steers quantitative parameters within hardcoded ranges and votes on permitted onboarding decisions. It cannot reach the immutable core: the bonding curve, CDP logic, liquidations, redemptions, and Stability Pool mechanics are beyond any vote.

vePOLAR holders can participate in decisions to:

- License new pAssets onto Polaris infrastructure.
- Onboard eligible participants into StablecoinOS and [Flow](/stewardship/flows) revenue sharing.
- Set bounded interest splits and CDP parameters.
- Tune [conversion](/polar/conversion-auctions) parameters within their hardcoded range.
- Vote on treasury programs once the framework is published.

Each action is bounded, auditable, and limited to the surfaces in [Stewardship, not Governance](/stewardship/stewardship). None can denature the protocol.

## What vePOLAR does not do

vePOLAR does not:

- Direct POLAR emissions.
- Create gauges or bribe markets.
- Upgrade core contracts.
- Change pETH collateral mechanics.
- Override liquidations, redemptions, or Stability Pool rules.
- Mint POLAR outside the pETH conversion mechanism.
- Move a parameter outside its immutable min/max range.

## Yield for lockers

vePOLAR is not influence alone. Lockers are one destination of distributed protocol yield: a stewarded share of protocol interest is routed to POLAR stakers, alongside the pETH the [Fee Router](/stewardship/fee-router) distributes to troves.

The exact split, buffering, and claim mechanics are pending final deployment. This is routed protocol revenue, not POLAR emissions.

## Prerequisites for locking

Before locking POLAR, verify:

- The official app URL.
- POLAR and vePOLAR addresses against [Contracts and Addresses](/resources/contracts).
- Verified source and ABI against [Contracts and Addresses](/resources/contracts).
- Lock duration, voting power, unlock, and withdrawal rules.
- The final yield split and claim path.
- The audit status of the locking and stewardship contracts.

Locking is an illiquid commitment by design. Do not lock through any contract or frontend not published in official Polaris docs.

## The Polar Council

The **Polar Council** is a treasury-funded program to bootstrap aligned, active stewards. Comparable in spirit to veAERO's Flight School, it is a structured way to cultivate participants who understand the protocol and contribute to its stewardship, rather than leaving the role to whoever shows up.

> **Current status:** The Council's operating framework is initially team-managed and will be formalized as providers step up and launch approaches. Exact terms, eligibility, and reporting requirements are pending.

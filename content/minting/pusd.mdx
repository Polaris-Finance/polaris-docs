# pUSD

pUSD is Polaris's flagship **pAsset pegged to the US dollar**, issued against pETH collateral. It belongs to a rare class of stablecoins that carry **no counterparty dependency** - the same trust-minimized family as LUSD and BOLD. Its peg is not propped up by an offchain reserve that can be frozen or fail; it is held by onchain incentives.

> **Note:** pUSD is Testnet-active on Sepolia. See [Launch Status](/launch-status) for the current list of live pAssets.

## Why use pUSD

pUSD's yield is **owned, not rented**. Where most stablecoin yield is borrowed from offchain sources (T-bills, CEX lending, custodial strategies) that can compress or disappear, pUSD earns from the protocol's own activity - borrower interest and liquidation gains - so it scales with adoption rather than external conditions. See [Yield Sources](/yield/yield-sources#owned-not-rented) for why this is self-scaling.

## How pUSD is created

You issue pUSD by depositing pETH collateral and opening a position - the same flow as any pAsset. Arbitrageurs can also issue or redeem pUSD directly against pETH at the oracle price through the Peg Stability Module.

- Issue from a position: [Issuing pAssets](/minting) → [Open a Position](/minting/open-a-trove).
- Direct PSM issue/redeem: [Advanced](/using-app/advanced).

## How the peg is supported

Two opposing, permissionless arbitrage mechanisms defend the peg without anyone's approval:

- **Below peg**, [redemptions](/redemptions-liquidations) let anyone swap pUSD for its pETH collateral at face value, buying pUSD off the market until it returns to peg.
- **Above peg**, issuing new pUSD against pETH expands supply back toward peg.

For the full treatment, see [Redemptions](/redemptions-liquidations) and the peg section of the [Risk Disclosure](/resources/risk-disclosure).

## Earn with pUSD

Deposit pUSD into the [Earn Vault](/yield) to earn borrower interest plus pETH liquidation gains, without opening a borrow position of your own. The vault is a first-loss backstop - read [Earn Vaults](/yield) before depositing.

## Main risks

pUSD carries collateral, oracle, redemption, and smart-contract risk. Read the [Risk Disclosure](/resources/risk-disclosure) before treating pUSD yield as principal-safe.

# pAssets Overview

A **pAsset** is a synthetic debt asset issued directly from the Polaris protocol against pETH collateral. **pETH is different:** it is the bonding-curve collateral token that backs pAssets, not a debt asset issued from a borrow position.

> **Current Public Testnet 1 focus:** pUSD and pGOLD are Testnet-active on Sepolia. See [Launch Status](/launch-status) for the live list.

## Live pAssets

| Asset | Tracks | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **pUSD** | US dollar | **Testnet-active** | The flagship pAsset. See [pUSD](/minting/pusd). |
| **pGOLD** | 1 ounce of gold | **Testnet-active** | Decentralized gold exposure, backed by pETH rather than bullion. See [pGOLD](/minting/pgold). |
| **pETH** | ETH | **Testnet-active collateral** | *Special.* Not issued from a borrow position: it is the collateral itself, produced by the [bonding curve](/peth). It backs every other pAsset. |

## New pAsset markets

Polaris is **debt-asset agnostic**: it can issue a synthetic pegged to any asset with robust reference pricing and risk parameters, and external teams can build new pAsset markets on the shared pETH collateral base. See [Launch a pAsset](/minting/launch-a-passet).

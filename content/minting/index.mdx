# Polaris Minting and Troves

Minting means opening a trove, posting pETH collateral, and drawing a pAsset directly from the protocol. Production minting is not mainnet-actionable until [Launch Status](/launch-status) publishes the app, verified addresses, final parameters, and audit status.

## Launch status

For Testnet 2, use Sepolia (`11155111`) and the current testnet deployment manifests. For production, wait for Ethereum mainnet (`1`) contracts and final borrower parameters.

# Polaris Minting and Troves

Minting means opening a trove, posting pETH collateral, and drawing a pAsset directly from the protocol. Production minting is not mainnet-actionable until [Launch Status](/launch-status) publishes the app, verified addresses, final parameters, and audit status.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Status">
Public Testnet 1 exposes pUSD and pGOLD flows on Sepolia. Production borrower flows remain pending until Launch Status publishes mainnet contracts and final parameters.
</Callout>

## Launch status

For Public Testnet 1, use Sepolia (`11155111`) and the current testnet deployment manifests. For production, wait for Ethereum mainnet (`1`) contracts and final borrower parameters.

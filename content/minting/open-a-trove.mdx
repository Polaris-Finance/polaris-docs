# Open a Borrow Position

Use the **Borrow** tab to issue pUSD or pGOLD against pETH collateral. The app shows the position preview before you sign: collateral, issued amount, loan-to-value (LTV), projected liquidation price, estimated monthly cost, and effective rate.

![Polaris Borrow tab with pUSD, pGOLD, and ETH sub-tabs, a collateral field, LTV slider, effective rate, and a projected-position panel](/screenshots/borrow.jpg)

*Borrow opens and manages pAsset debt positions. Review the projected-position panel before signing.*

## Read the screen

<Callout variant="info" title="Collateral">
For pUSD and pGOLD, the collateral is pETH. If you need pETH first, use [Swap](/using-app/swap) to buy it on the bonding curve.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="info" title="Borrow amount">
Choose the pAsset sub-tab, enter collateral, then use the LTV slider or amount field to set how much pUSD or pGOLD to issue.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="LTV">
LTV is the main risk signal. Keep enough room below the maximum to absorb ETH price moves, interest accrual, gas delays, and redemption impact.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="info" title="Preview">
Before signing, compare the projected liquidation price, estimated monthly cost, effective rate, and resulting LTV with the position you intended to open.
</Callout>

## Open the position

1. Open [Borrow](/using-app/borrow).
2. Select **pUSD** or **pGOLD**.
3. Enter pETH collateral and the amount to issue.
4. Review the projected position.
5. Approve pETH if prompted, then sign the borrow transaction.
6. Return to the Dashboard or Borrow tab and confirm the position appears with the expected collateral, debt, and LTV.

## First testnet flow

1. Get a small amount of Sepolia ETH from a public Sepolia faucet for gas.
2. Open [app.testnet.polarisfinance.io](https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/) and connect a wallet on Sepolia (`11155111`).
3. Use the app's reserve-token faucet if it is enabled for your wallet. The deployment manifest names the reserve token `WETH`; the prototype app may display it as ETH for readability. It is testnet-only and has no value.
4. Use the bonding-curve / swap surface to exchange test reserve tokens for pETH.
5. Borrow or mint a Testnet-active pAsset such as pUSD or pGOLD where the app surface supports it.
6. Optionally deposit the pAsset into the Stability Pool to test the Earn flow.

If the app faucet is disabled for your wallet, do not send mainnet ETH or mainnet WETH. Wait for the published testnet faucet path or use only the assets explicitly supported by the app.

## Before you sign

Check all of these before any Testnet 2 transaction:

- The URL is exactly [https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/](https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/).
- Your wallet network is Sepolia and the chain ID is `11155111`.
- The asset is a test asset and has no monetary value.
- The contract address appears on this page or in the linked Sepolia manifest.
- The app quote shows the amount, fee, and asset you expect.
- You are not sending mainnet ETH, mainnet WETH, or any other real-value asset.

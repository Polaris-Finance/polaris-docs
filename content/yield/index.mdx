# Polaris Yield

Polaris yield is intended to come from onchain protocol activity, not token emissions or offchain counterparties. Production yield actions are not mainnet-actionable until [Launch Status](/launch-status) publishes official contracts and parameters.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Yield is not a promise">
APR and reward estimates are live-state estimates. Realized yield depends on borrower interest, liquidation events, pETH price movement, fees, gas, and whether wrapper vaults are available.
</Callout>

# Polaris Yield Sources

Polaris yield comes from onchain protocol activity: borrowing, liquidations, swaps, and conversions. This page lists the sources; [Fee Router & Flows](/stewardship/fee-router) covers routing.

> Adoption ⇒ activity ⇒ velocity ⇒ fees ⇒ yield ⇒ more demand.

## The sources

| Source | Where it originates | Flows to |
|---|---|---|
| **Borrower interest** | Interest paid by positions that issued pAssets | Split by the interest router between [Earn Vault](/yield) depositors, POLAR stakers, and an ecosystem remainder |
| **Liquidation gains** | Penalties on liquidated positions, paid in discounted pETH | Earn Vault depositors |
| **Bonding curve swap fees** | Fees on every ETH to pETH or pETH to ETH swap | The pETH floor and positions that receive routed pETH rewards |
| **Conversion gains** | ETH released when pETH is burned to create POLAR in a [conversion auction](/polar/conversion-auctions) | The Fee Router, which recycles it into pETH and streams it to positions based on recorded debt |

The first two drive [Earn Vault](/yield) yield and are the primary venue for pAsset holders. The bonding curve and conversion components make **pETH used as collateral yield-bearing**: yield accrues to positions and to pETH's rising floor, not to a passive token balance.

## Why this is "self-scaling"

These sources depend on protocol **activity**:

- More positions opened means more borrower interest.
- More volatility can mean more liquidation gains.
- More trading means more bonding curve swap fees.
- More POLAR created through conversion means more conversion gains.

Yield scales with pAsset supply and protocol use, but realized returns still depend on activity, rates, liquidations, contract execution, and market conditions.

## Owned, not rented

Polaris yield is not subsidized, bridged in, or voted into being. It emerges from protocol use and is captured by the protocol itself.

## How yield is distributed

Conversion proceeds and swap fees go through the **[Fee Router](/stewardship/fee-router)**. Borrower interest follows separate pAsset-denominated split logic. Current values and pending production status live on [Parameters](/resources/testnet#parameters).

# POLAR Token, Staking, and Conversion

POLAR is Polaris's stewardship token and outward-facing growth lever. After the initial distribution, new POLAR is intended to enter through one path: [burn pETH in a conversion auction](/polar/conversion-auctions).

<Callout variant="info" title="Where this appears in the app">
App anchors: **Earn** for current POLAR Staking where exposed, and **Advanced** or a conversion panel for pETH -> POLAR conversion actions.
</Callout>

> **Status distinction:** The app may expose **POLAR Staking** as a current stake/unstake/claim surface on testnet. **vePOLAR locking** is separate and forthcoming - see [POLAR Staking vs vePOLAR](/polar/tokenomics#polar-staking-vs-vepolar) for what is committed and what is pending. Do not use a conversion, staking, or locking interface unless [Launch Status](/launch-status) and [Contracts & Addresses](/resources/testnet#contracts-and-addresses) point to it.

## Why a third token

Polaris runs on three engines: pETH, the pAssets, and POLAR. The first two handle everything the protocol *needs* to function: pETH backs pAsset markets and user positions, Earn Vaults generate yield, interest rates self-adjust, and liquidations process autonomously. **Polaris works without POLAR.**

So why add a third token? Because functioning and scaling differ. The design splits along an **inward- vs. outward-facing** logic:

| Function | Handled by | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Inward-facing** - sustain the core | pETH + pAssets | interest rates, floor growth, Earn Vault incentivization, liquidations |
| **Outward-facing** - grow the ecosystem | POLAR | external DEX liquidity, Flow onboarding, [StablecoinOS](/minting/launch-a-passet#stablecoinos-an-open-catalog) coordination |

POLAR is deliberately separated from the core so the core's sustainability never depends on it. If POLAR incentives dried up tomorrow, pUSD could still be issued, the bonding curve would still function, and Earn Vaults would still pay yield. The base protocol does not need POLAR to survive, but it scales far better with it.

## Created only by burning pETH

There is no planned team creation function or calendar inflation schedule disconnected from protocol activity. The detailed economics live on [POLAR Tokenomics](/polar/tokenomics); the auction mechanics live on [Conversion Auctions](/polar/conversion-auctions).

## POLAR Staking and future vePOLAR

POLAR can be put to work two ways: current **POLAR Staking** where exposed, and forthcoming **[vePOLAR](/stewardship/vepolar)** locking for stewardship rights. See [POLAR Tokenomics](/polar/tokenomics#polar-staking-vs-vepolar) for the full distinction.

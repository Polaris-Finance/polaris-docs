# Polaris POLAR, Staking, and Conversion

POLAR is the stewardship token. After the initial distribution, new POLAR is intended to enter only when pETH is burned through conversion, not through emissions.

> **Status distinction:** The app may expose **POLAR Staking** as a current stake/unstake/claim surface on testnet. **vePOLAR locking** is separate and forthcoming: lock durations, vote-power decay, voting rules, and production addresses are pending audited deployment.

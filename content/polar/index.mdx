# POLAR Token, Stewardship, and Conversion

## POLAR

POLAR is the stewardship and growth token of Polaris.

The core protocol is built to function through pETH and pAssets.

pETH backs the system, pAssets provide decentralized currencies and synthetic assets, and Earn Vaults help absorb liquidations while earning from protocol activity.

POLAR exists for a different purpose: coordination, growth, and long-term stewardship.

## Why POLAR Exists

Polaris separates the protocol core from the token used to grow and coordinate the ecosystem.

The core protocol should not depend on emissions to survive.

pAssets can be created, the bonding curve can operate, and Earn Vaults can earn from protocol activity without relying on POLAR incentives.

POLAR exists to help Polaris scale beyond the core.

It can support:

- External liquidity.
- Ecosystem incentives.
- Flow onboarding.
- Protocol integrations.
- Future stewardship through vePOLAR.

## Created By Burning pETH

After the initial distribution, new POLAR is intended to enter through one primary path: converting pETH into POLAR.

This conversion is one-way and cannot be reversed.

When users convert pETH into POLAR, pETH is burned. Burning pETH reduces supply, generates yield that is directed via the pETH flows and helps raise the pETH floor for remaining pETH holders.

That means POLAR issuance is tied to activity that strengthens the collateral base, rather than a fixed emissions schedule disconnected from protocol usage.

For the full mechanism, see [Conversion Auctions](/polar/conversion-auctions).

## Productive Dilution

POLAR issuance is dilution, but it is designed to be productive dilution.

New POLAR is created when pETH is burned.

That burn raises the pETH floor and improves the reserve foundation that supports Polaris.

The goal is not to emit tokens for their own sake, but to exchange pETH burns for ecosystem growth, liquidity, and stewardship alignment.

It also captures demand for POLAR and converts it into yield and value capture for the broader protocol.

## vePOLAR Stewardship

POLAR's long-term role is stewardship through vePOLAR.

vePOLAR is the forthcoming vote-escrow layer for Polaris.

Users will lock POLAR to receive stewardship rights and participate in bounded protocol coordination.

The goal is not open-ended governance over the core protocol.

The core remains constrained. vePOLAR is designed to coordinate parameters, incentives, integrations, and ecosystem growth within defined limits.

Current testnet staking surfaces are temporary and should not be treated as part of the final POLAR design.

For live app surfaces and contract status, use [Launch Status](/launch-status) and [Contracts And Addresses](/resources/testnet#contracts-and-addresses).

## Stewardship Without Mutable Core Logic

POLAR is designed to coordinate the ecosystem without making the core protocol dependent on open-ended governance.

The core remains constrained.

vePOLAR stewardship is intended to coordinate parameters, incentives, integrations, and ecosystem growth within defined limits.

This gives Polaris a path to adapt and scale while preserving the immutability of the base system.

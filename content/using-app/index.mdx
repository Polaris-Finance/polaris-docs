# Using the Polaris App

The app is organized around task labels. Use this section when you know the label you see in the app and want the matching docs page.

> **Network status:** Public testnet flows use Sepolia (`11155111`) and test assets. Production Ethereum mainnet flows are not actionable until [Launch Status](/launch-status) publishes the production app, addresses, parameters, and audits.

## App labels

| App label | Use it for | Start here |
|---|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Account balances, open positions, claimable rewards, and system status | [Dashboard](/using-app/dashboard) |
| **Swap** | WETH/pETH/pAsset swaps and bonding-curve quotes | [Swap](/using-app/swap) |
| **Borrow** | pAsset troves and reserve loans | [Borrow](/using-app/borrow) |

| **Advanced** | Manual approvals, PSM/redemption tools, reserve loans, and staking controls | [Advanced](/using-app/advanced) |
| **Analytics** | Protocol metrics, floor ratio, rates, liquidity, and pool health | [Analytics](/using-app/analytics) |

## Before any transaction

Before signing in any app tab, verify:

- You are on Sepolia for Public Testnet 1, or Ethereum mainnet only after production launch.
- The app URL and contracts match [Launch Status](/launch-status) and [Contracts & Addresses](/resources/contracts).
- The token, amount, fee, slippage limit, quote freshness, and resulting position are what you intended.
- Any approval is limited to the contract and token shown in the app.
- You understand the risk of the specific action: debt, liquidation, first-loss exposure, one-way conversion, or pETH floor/execution assumptions.

For broader wallet and contract checks, use [Safety and Verification](/paths/safety-verification).

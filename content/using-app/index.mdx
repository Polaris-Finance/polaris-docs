# Using the Polaris App

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 2026" />

<Callout variant="success" title="Open the Testnet App">
The app runs at [app.testnet.polarisfinance.io](https://app.testnet.polarisfinance.io/) on **Sepolia testnet** (`11155111`) with test assets only — they have no monetary value. Production/mainnet is not live until [Launch Status](/launch-status) says otherwise.
</Callout>

The app is organized around task labels. Use this section when you know the label you see in the app and want the matching docs page.

## App labels

| App label | Use it for | Start here |
|---|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Account balances, open positions, claimable rewards, and system status | [Dashboard & Analytics](/using-app/dashboard) |
| **Swap** | WETH/pETH/pAsset swaps and bonding-curve quotes | [Swap](/using-app/swap) |
| **Borrow** | pAsset troves and reserve loans | [Borrow](/using-app/borrow) |
| **Earn** | Stability Pool deposits and staking rewards | [Earn](/using-app/earn) |
| **Split** | Split pETH into fpETH and vpETH, or merge them back | [Split](/using-app/split) |
| **Zap** | Bundle a purchase and Stability Pool deposit into one guided flow | [Zap](/using-app/zap) |
| **Guide** | Guided routing by goal | [Choose by goal](#choose-by-goal) |
| **Advanced** | Manual approvals, PSM/redemption tools, reserve loans, and staking controls | [Advanced](/using-app/advanced) |
| **Analytics** | Protocol metrics, floor ratio, rates, liquidity, and pool health | [Dashboard & Analytics](/using-app/dashboard#analytics-protocol-wide-metrics) |

## Choose by goal

When you know the outcome you want but not which tab to start in:

| Goal | App tab | Start with |
|---|---|---|
| Get pETH | Swap | [pETH & Bonding Curve](/peth) |
| Mint pUSD or pGOLD | Borrow | [Minting & Troves](/minting) |
| Deposit into the Stability Pool | Earn or Zap | [Earning Yield](/yield) |
| Split pETH into fpETH and vpETH | Split | [pETH Split](/peth/split) |
| Convert pETH into POLAR | Advanced or conversion panel | [POLAR & Staking](/polar) |
| Verify before interacting | Dashboard, then any action tab | [Safety and Verification](/paths/safety-verification) |

Read the linked page before signing, then return to the app tab for the live quote and simulation — a goal router cannot verify that a quote is fresh or that an address is official.

## Before any transaction

Before signing in any app tab, verify:

- You are on Sepolia for Public Testnet 1, or Ethereum mainnet only after production launch.
- The app URL and contracts match [Launch Status](/launch-status) and [Contracts & Addresses](/resources/contracts).
- The token, amount, fee, slippage limit, quote freshness, and resulting position are what you intended.
- Any approval is limited to the contract and token shown in the app.
- You understand the risk of the specific action: debt, liquidation, first-loss exposure, one-way conversion, or pETH floor/execution assumptions.

For broader wallet and contract checks, use [Safety and Verification](/paths/safety-verification).

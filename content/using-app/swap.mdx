# Swap

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 9, 2026" />

## What it does

Swap is the bonding-curve interface for pETH. Sell ETH (WETH) to **buy** pETH, or sell pETH back for ETH — the route is always `WETH → bonding curve → pETH`. Each mint locks ETH into the curve's reserve at the current marginal price, and pETH carries a hard, ever-rising price floor. To get fpETH or vpETH use [Split](/using-app/split); to mint pUSD or pGOLD use [Borrow](/using-app/borrow).

## Before you start

- Start from the [Dashboard](/using-app) and verify the current app and network first.
- Hold WETH (claim it from the faucet) and enough Sepolia ETH for gas.
- Read the direction, output, minimum received, price impact, and approval target before signing.

## Steps/checks

1. Choose the direction: buy pETH with WETH, or sell pETH for WETH.
2. Enter the amount and review the quote and route.
3. Check minimum received, price impact, fees, and slippage.
4. Approve the input token only if the spender matches the app flow.
5. Sign the swap transaction.
6. Confirm the pETH (or WETH) balance updates in the wallet.

The swap worked when the transaction lands and the output token balance updates.

## Common failures

- Quote expired before the transaction landed.
- Slippage limit was too tight.
- Input balance or allowance was too low.
- Gas was too low or Sepolia was congested.
- Wallet is connected to the wrong account or network.

## Risks/links

Swaps can lose value through slippage, price movement, stale quotes, and gas. pETH mechanics are explained in [pETH](/peth) and [pETH Floor Price](/peth/floor-price). Use [Risk Disclosure](/resources/risk-disclosure) for risk details.

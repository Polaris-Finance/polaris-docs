# Swap In The Polaris App

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 12, 2026" />

Swap provides a simple way to exchange between supported Polaris assets. Use it to acquire pETH, move between pAssets, or swap into supported test assets when available.

As the collateral foundation of the ecosystem, pETH is required for actions such as issuing pAssets, splitting into fpETH and vpETH, and accessing other protocol strategies. pETH swaps route through the Polaris bonding curve.

## Basic Flow

1. Choose the direction: buy pETH with WETH, or sell pETH for WETH.
2. Enter the amount.
3. Review the quote and route.
4. Check minimum received, fees, and slippage.
5. Approve the input token if prompted.
6. Sign the swap transaction.
7. Confirm your pETH or WETH balance updates.

For stale quotes or reverted swaps, see [Troubleshooting](/troubleshooting).

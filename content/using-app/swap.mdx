# Swap

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 2026" />

Use **Swap** when you want to exchange WETH, pETH, fpETH, vpETH, or a pAsset through the route the app quotes. For pETH, the route may involve the [bonding curve](/peth/bonding-curve); for pAssets, it may route through app-supported markets or protocol modules.

<Callout variant="info" title="Where this appears in the app">
App anchor: **Swap** tab. Use it for WETH -> pETH, pETH -> WETH, pAsset routes, and any quoted route that shows price impact or slippage before signing.
</Callout>

![Polaris Swap tab: selling 5 ETH for pETH, showing the quoted output, minimum received, exchange rate, and a route of WETH through the bonding curve to pETH](/screenshots/swap.jpg)

*The quote shows output, minimum received, and rate; the route panel makes the path explicit — here WETH through the bonding curve to pETH.*

## Common swaps

| Goal | Typical route | Read first |
|---|---|---|
| Get pETH | WETH -> pETH | [pETH Bonding Curve](/peth/bonding-curve) |
| Exit pETH | pETH -> WETH | [pETH Floor Price](/peth/floor-price) |
| Get a pAsset to deposit | WETH -> pUSD or pGOLD | [Polaris Asset Catalog](/minting/passet-catalog) |
| Rebalance before a debt action | pAsset or pETH swap | [Managing a Trove](/minting/managing-your-trove) |


## Risks

Swapping does not remove pETH price risk, pAsset peg risk, or execution risk. The final amount can differ from the preview because of gas, slippage, MEV, route changes, and transaction ordering.

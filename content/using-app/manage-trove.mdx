# Manage Trove

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 8, 2026" />

## What it does

Manage Trove is the ongoing maintenance flow for an existing pAsset debt position. Use it to add collateral, mint more, repay debt, withdraw collateral when allowed, close the trove, or inspect liquidation exposure.

## Before you start

- Start from [Dashboard & Analytics](/using-app) and verify the current app/network first.
- Connect the wallet that owns the trove.
- Check the current ICR, debt, collateral, borrow rate, and Recovery Mode state.
- Use [Parameters](/resources/parameters) for current thresholds and [Managing Your Trove](/minting/managing-your-trove) for mechanics.

## Steps/checks

1. Open the existing trove from Dashboard or Borrow.
2. Choose the action: add collateral, mint more, repay, withdraw collateral, or close.
3. Review the resulting ICR and whether the action increases or reduces risk.
4. Confirm allowances if the action spends pETH or pAssets.
5. Sign the transaction.
6. Return to Dashboard and confirm collateral, debt, and ICR updated.

The action worked when the trove state matches the intended collateral and debt change.

## Common failures

- The connected wallet does not own the trove.
- Repay amount exceeds your pAsset balance.
- Withdraw or mint would take ICR below the required threshold.
- Recovery Mode blocks risk-increasing actions.
- Interest accrued between quote and inclusion.
- A redemption changed the trove before your transaction landed.

## Risks/links

A healthy ICR protects against liquidation, not against proportional redemptions. Use [Redemptions](/redemptions-liquidations), [Liquidations](/redemptions-liquidations/liquidations), [Recovery Mode](/redemptions-liquidations/recovery-mode), and [Risk Disclosure](/resources/risk-disclosure) for the risk model.

# Earn In The Polaris App

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 12, 2026" />

Earn is where you deposit assets into Polaris yield opportunities.

Current opportunities include:
- pUSD Earn Vault, where deposits earn from issuer interest and liquidation gains.
- pGOLD Earn Vault, where deposits earn from issuer interest and liquidation gains.
- vpETH rewards, where holders gain exposure to the volatile component of pETH beyond its floor value and participate in pETH rewards distribution.

Earn Vault deposits act as the first line of defense during liquidations. In exchange, depositors receive yield from protocol activity.

Current testnet POLAR staking surfaces are temporary and should not be treated as part of the final POLAR design.

![Polaris Earn tab with pUSD, pGOLD, and vpETH sub-tabs, APRs, deposit/withdraw actions, and claim-pending options](/screenshots/earn.jpg)

## Basic Flow

1. Select the sub-tab.
2. Review its APR, your current deposit, and pending gains.
3. Choose deposit, withdraw, or claim where available.
4. Approve the token if required.
5. Sign the transaction.
6. Confirm your deposit, balance, and claimable gains updated.

For disabled deposits, claims, or rewards actions, see [Troubleshooting](/troubleshooting).

For first-loss risk, see [Risk Disclosure](/resources/risk-disclosure).

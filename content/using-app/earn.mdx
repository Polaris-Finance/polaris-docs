# Earn In The Polaris App

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 12, 2026" />

Earn is where you deposit assets into Polaris yield opportunities.

Current opportunities include:

- USDp Earn Vault, where depositors earn from issuer interest and liquidation gains.
- GOLDp Earn Vault, where deposits earn from issuer interest and liquidation gains.
- vpETH rewards, where holders gain exposure to the volatile component of pETH and participate in pETH rewards distribution.

Earn Vault deposits help process liquidations and support the stability of the system during periods of stress. In return, depositors earn protocol-native yield.

Current testnet POLAR staking mechanics are temporary and might not reflect the final POLAR design.

## Basic Flow

1. Select the sub-tab.
2. Review its APR, your current deposit, and pending gains.
3. Choose deposit, withdraw, or claim where available.
4. Approve the token if required.
5. Sign the transaction.
6. Confirm your deposit, balance, and claimable gains updated.

For disabled deposits, claims, or rewards actions, see [Troubleshooting](/troubleshooting).

For Earn Vault mechanics, see [Earn Vaults](/yield).

# Borrow

Use **Borrow** for two distinct position types:

<Callout variant="info" title="Where this appears in the app">
App anchor: **Borrow** tab. Trove actions appear as pAsset borrowing/CDP actions; reserve loans appear as fpETH-backed WETH loan actions.
</Callout>

| App wording | Docs wording | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| **Borrow pAssets** | **Open or adjust a trove** | Deposit pETH collateral and mint pUSD, pGOLD, or another pAsset as debt. |
| **CDP** | **Trove / CDP instance** | The contract mechanism behind a pAsset borrow position. |
| **Loan / Reserve Loan** | **Reserve loan** | Borrow WETH against fpETH floor-value collateral; this is not a pAsset trove. |

Docs use **trove** for a pAsset borrowing position because each position has its own collateral, debt, and ICR. The app or contracts may also say **Borrow** or **CDP**. A **reserve loan** is separate: it uses [fpETH](/peth/split) and the bonding-curve reserve, not pAsset debt.

## Before you sign

- Confirm whether you are opening a pAsset trove or a reserve loan.
- Confirm the network, app URL, and relevant manager contract.
- For a trove, check collateral amount, minted pAsset amount, ICR, minimum ICR, Recovery Mode status, borrow APR, and redemption exposure.
- For a reserve loan, check fpETH collateral, WETH debt, max borrow amount, accrued debt behavior, performance fee, and close/repay requirements.
- Check all approvals for pETH, fpETH, WETH, or pAssets.
- Simulate the transaction and refresh the quote if floor price, borrow rate, or oracle state changed.

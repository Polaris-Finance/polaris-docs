# Borrow

<PageStatusBanner lastUpdated="June 2026" />

Use **Borrow** for two distinct position types:

<Callout variant="info" title="Where this appears in the app">
App anchor: **Borrow** tab. Trove actions appear as pAsset borrowing/CDP actions; reserve loans appear as fpETH-backed WETH loan actions.
</Callout>

![Polaris Borrow tab: pUSD selected, 10 pETH collateral at 75% LTV, with a projected position showing liquidation price, estimated monthly cost, and LTV](/screenshots/borrow.jpg)

*Pick the pAsset to mint, set collateral and LTV, then review the projected position — liquidation price, monthly cost, and LTV — before approving.*

| App wording | Docs wording | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| **Borrow pAssets** | **Open or adjust a trove** | Deposit pETH collateral and mint pUSD, pGOLD, or another pAsset as debt. |
| **CDP** | **Trove / CDP instance** | The contract mechanism behind a pAsset borrow position. |
| **Loan / Reserve Loan** | **Reserve loan** | Borrow WETH against fpETH floor-value collateral; this is not a pAsset trove. |

For the full **Borrow / CDP / trove / reserve loan** vocabulary, see [Borrower terminology](/minting/minting-passets#borrower-terminology).

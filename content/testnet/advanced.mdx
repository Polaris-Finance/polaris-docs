# Advanced

Advanced provides direct access to protocol mechanisms that are primarily intended for arbitrageurs, keepers and experienced users.

This section includes tools such as the Peg Stability Module, the pETH-to-POLAR conversion mechanism and the testnet faucet.

- Peg Stability Module: Mint or redeem USDp and GOLDp against pETH at the oracle price.
- Convert pETH to POLAR: Convert pETH into newly issued POLAR through the protocol's one-way conversion mechanism.

While these actions remain permissionless, they assume a good understanding of how Polaris works.

If you are unfamiliar with a particular mechanism, refer to its dedicated documentation before using it.

# Earn

This section gives users access to the Earn Vaults. From here you can deposit into the auto-compounding sUSDp and sGOLDp Earn Vaults, monitor your deposits and accumulated yield, and withdraw your funds whenever you want.

Everything related to your Earn positions is managed from this section.

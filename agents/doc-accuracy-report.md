### The single most important finding

> 🔴 **The documentation describes Liquity-style redemptions ("redemptions hit the lowest-ICR troves first; walk up the queue from the riskiest trove"). The code implements the opposite: a _collective, socialized_ redemption.** A redemption burns pAsset and removes pETH **pro-rata across _every_ open trove in proportion to its collateral** — there is no sorted list, no ICR ordering, and no "queue." Consequently the docs' central borrower advice — *"keep a higher ICR to stay out of redemption range"* — **is wrong**: a higher ICR protects against _liquidation_, not redemption. Every open position is redeemed against proportionally, regardless of ICR.

**Code:** `PegStabilityModule.redeem()` (`src/PegStabilityModule.sol:136`) burns the user's stables and makes a single `cdpManager.distribute(-coll, -stables, user)` call (`:155`); `CDPManager._distribute()` (`src/CDPManager.sol:650`) updates the global `mintRedeemCollProd` / `mintRedeemDebtSum` accumulators once — comment at `:654`: *"Mints and redeems are pro-rata to recorded coll."* No trove id, no loop, no ICR comparison anywhere in the path.

## Verified accurate (no change needed)

The following load-bearing claims were checked and **match the code** — worth stating so the report is not read as wholesale distrust:

- **pETH floor price only rises.** `floorSupply`/`floorBalance` move only "upwards (right)" via `floorSellAndBurn`/`buyFloorSellAndBurn`; the floor is monotonic by construction.
- **Conversion is one-way, spike-and-decay, with a converter rebate.** `ExponentialDutchAuction` (decay) + `SupplyInflationPriceSpike` (spike vs POLAR supply) + `rebatePct` of freed ETH reserve; there is no POLAR→pETH path.
- **POLAR is minted only by burning pETH _after the initial distribution_.** `tokenomics.mdx`'s caveated wording is correct (the explainers' uncaveated version is not — Theme E).
- **Autonomous, market-aware interest with no pAsset price feed.** `PSM.mint/redeem` feed mint/redeem volume to `interestController.updatePrimaryInterestRate(...)`; rates respond to redemption/mint pressure, not an external pUSD oracle.
- **Collateral is pETH and only pETH.** `CDPManager.collToken` is the bonding-curve token; no staked-ETH or external collateral path.
- **The Stability Pool can never be fully emptied by liquidations** (`MIN_STABLE_TOKENS_IN_SP = 1e18`), so `P` never reaches 0 (`Constants.sol`).
- **Two-tier liquidation:** Stability-Pool offset first, then redistribution to remaining troves (`calcOffsetValues`, `CDPManagerMath.sol`).
- **Cross-pAsset isolation:** each pAsset is its own CDPManager + Stability Pool; one pAsset's debt cannot reach another's troves.
- **The immutable core is out of stewardship reach:** `MCR`, `EMERGENCY_MODE_MCR`, `EMERGENCY_MODE_RATIO` are `immutable`; the bonding curve, CDP, liquidation, and redemption logic are not steward-settable.
- **Deliberately pre-launch content is correct, not an error:** `contracts.mdx`, `contract-reference.mdx`, `integration-guide.mdx`, `launch-readiness.mdx` keep addresses, ABIs, event signatures, and final parameter bounds as *TBA/forthcoming*. This candor matches the testnet state and should be preserved.
